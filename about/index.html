András Nagy is digital media artist, living in Hungary. He started working as a VJ and sound engineer in 2013, contributed on a dozen various musical events, later in 2014 he worked at Mafilm Audio dubbing studio. In 2015 he switched to larger scale projects, such as building projections and live visuals for major concerts. He uses a broad variety of techniques, from hand drawn elements to procedurally created shapes. The main thing about his work is that everything is generated live through patching.
