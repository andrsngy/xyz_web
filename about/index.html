BIOGRAPHY

András László Nagy completed his studies in Budapest in the fields of drawing and sound technology. Over the past ten years, his work has primarily focused on creating light installations and real-time animations.
In his projects, he pays special attention to the relationship between image and sound, as well as their interaction within the world of digital art and interactive technologies.
He has conducted numerous international workshops on using the TouchDesigner software, sharing his experience and knowledge in visual programming and generative art. The functioning of light and its creative applications have always been a key area of interest for him, which he complements with practical aspects of electronics, 3D printing, and programming.
In his work, he strives to merge artistic and technological approaches, constantly seeking new opportunities and innovative solutions in the field of visual and audiovisual expression.

In 2021, together with his creative team, he won the Master of Light award, founded by the Lisys-Project.
Since 2024, he has been a member of the International Kepes Society.
